The Process

From your first message
to a live website.

No technical jargon. No confusing back-and-forth. We've simplified everything into a process that works for busy business owners — not developers.

Step by Step

Your website in 4 stages

Most sites are live within 5–7 working days. Here's exactly what happens at every stage.

01

📋 Tell Us About Your Business

We start with a quick intake form or a WhatsApp chat — whatever is easier for you. You share your business name, what you do, what pages you want, and any design references or colors you like. This usually takes under 10 minutes and can be done right from your phone.

02

🎨 We Design It for You

Within 2–3 working days, our team designs a fully custom website based on your inputs. We choose fonts, colors, layouts, and content structure that match your business type. You don't need to provide any design files — we take care of that completely.

03

✅ You Review & Request Changes

We share a live preview link. You open it on your phone or laptop and give us your feedback — anything you'd like changed, text edits, color tweaks, layout adjustments. We offer unlimited revisions until you are 100% happy. Nothing goes live without your explicit approval.

04

🚀 We Launch It Live

Once approved, we deploy your website to a domain, configure hosting, and run final checks. You get a fully live website — shareable link, working on all devices, ready for customers. The entire journey from first message to live website typically takes 5–7 days.

Your Part

What we need
from you

✓

✓

✓
