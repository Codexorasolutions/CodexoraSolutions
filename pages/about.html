Our Story

Why we started
Codexora

Running a small business in India is hard. Getting a good website made it even harder — agencies quoted prices that only large companies could afford, timelines stretched for weeks, and the end result was often a generic template with a logo slapped on top.

We started Codexora to fix that. We believe a school principal in Ballia, a boutique owner in Kanpur, or a clinic in Varanasi deserves the same quality website as a big-city company — at a price that actually makes sense for their budget.

Every project we take is custom-built. No templates, no copy-paste. Just a website designed specifically for your business, delivered fast, and built to last.

What Drives Us

Our mission and
core values

🎯

Results First

Every design decision is made with one goal in mind: does this help your business convert visitors into customers? Aesthetics follow function, always.

🤝

Honest Pricing

We tell you the full cost upfront. No add-on surprises, no revision charges, no "that's extra" moments. If the quote changes, you hear it before we do any work.

⚡

Speed Without Compromise

We move fast — but never at the cost of quality. Our 7-day delivery comes from a tight, proven build process, not cutting corners on your website.

💬

Real Communication

You talk to the person building your site — not a sales agent, not a ticket system. WhatsApp, call, or email — we're always reachable and responsive.

🏅

Small Business Champions

Our entire model is designed around the needs of local businesses — straightforward processes, low minimums, and a style of working that fits busy owners.

🔑

Full Ownership

When the project is done, it's yours — completely. Code, domain, hosting, assets — we hand everything over. No lock-in, no dependency on us.
