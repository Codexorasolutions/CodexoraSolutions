Before You Start

Getting started

The basics — answered before you even need to ask.

How long does it take to build my website? +
Most websites are ready within 5–7 working days. For larger projects like college portals or multi-page shops, it may take 10–14 days. We always give you a clear timeline before we start — and we stick to it.
Do I need any technical knowledge to work with you? +
None at all. You don't need to know anything about websites, coding, or hosting. You just tell us what your business does, what you want, and we handle everything. Our entire process is designed for busy business owners — not developers.
Can I see examples of websites you've built? +
Absolutely. Visit our Work page to browse projects across education, retail, food, and healthcare. We're also happy to share additional examples over WhatsApp based on your specific business type.
How do I get started? +
The easiest way is to WhatsApp us at +91 96288 02518 with a quick description of your business and what you need. Alternatively, fill in our project form and we'll get back to you within a few hours. No commitment required at this stage.
Do you work with businesses outside your city? +
Absolutely. We work with clients across India — everything is done online via WhatsApp, email, and video call when needed. We've delivered projects for businesses in multiple states. Distance is never a barrier.
Design & Delivery

What we build and how

Can I provide my own design or layout preferences? +
Yes. If you have a design in mind, a reference website you like, or specific colors and fonts, just share them with us. We'll incorporate your preferences into the design. If you have no preference, we'll design something that suits your industry and looks professional.
What if I don't like the design? +
No problem. We offer unlimited revisions — if the first version doesn't feel right, just tell us what you'd like changed and we'll update it. We share a live preview before anything goes public, so you always have full control before launch.
Will my website look good on mobile phones? +
Every single website we build is fully responsive — meaning it adapts perfectly to all screen sizes including phones, tablets, and desktops. Since most customers browse on mobile, this is something we take extremely seriously and test on multiple devices before delivery.
Do you write the website content for me? +
We write basic placeholder content and page copy for you based on your business description. If you have your own text, we'll use that. For Premium clients, we can do more structured copywriting on request. Either way, you review all text before the site goes live.
Can I add features later after the website is built? +
Yes. You can upgrade your plan or add features like a gallery, blog section, booking form, or admin panel at any time after delivery. We provide a quote for additions and implement them quickly — usually within 2–3 days.
Pricing & Payment

Money questions

Do I need to buy a domain or hosting separately? +
Domain and hosting are purchased separately (they're third-party services). We guide you through the entire setup process or handle it on your behalf. Annual hosting typically costs ₹2,000–₹5,000/year and domain renewal is around ₹700–1,200/year depending on your domain name.
Is there any hidden charge after the website is delivered? +
Never. The price you see is the price you pay for the website build. Domain renewal and hosting are the only recurring costs, and we communicate these transparently before you sign up. There are no surprise invoices.
What are your payment terms? +
We typically work on a 50% advance, 50% on delivery model — so you only pay the remaining half once the website is complete and you're satisfied. For smaller projects, full payment upfront with a full refund guarantee may apply. We discuss this clearly before starting.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer (NEFT/IMPS), and Razorpay for card/net banking. Whatever is convenient for you — we're flexible.
After Launch

Ongoing support

Can I update the website myself after it's built? +
Yes — Premium plan clients get an admin dashboard to manage their own content. For all other clients, small updates like text changes, image swaps, pricing updates, or new notices can be done by WhatsApp-ing us. We turn most minor updates around within 24 hours.
What happens after my support period ends? +
You can continue with an AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) starting at ₹999/month for the Basic plan. Alternatively, you can reach out on a per-update basis and we'll quote you per change. Your website continues to run just fine regardless — support is about updates, not uptime.
What if my website goes down or breaks? +
Within your support period, we fix any issues at no charge. After the support period, minor technical fixes are handled quickly at a nominal charge. Critical site-down situations are always prioritized regardless of plan status — we won't leave you stranded.
Do you own any part of my website after delivery? +
No. Once delivered and paid for, the website is 100% yours — code, domain, hosting, content, everything. We sign no ownership agreement over your website. You can take it to any developer in the future if you choose to.
